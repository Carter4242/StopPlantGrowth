# Supported Plant Types

All supported plants can be locked/unlocked with shears. Locked plants block their growth behavior.

1. Vertical growers (upward)
- Sugar cane, cactus
- Bamboo (including bamboo sapling)
- Kelp (kelp + kelp plant)
- Twisting vines (twisting vines + twisting vines plant)
- Chorus plants (chorus plant + chorus flower)

2. Vertical growers (downward / spreading vines)
- Vines
- Weeping vines (weeping vines + weeping vines plant)
- Cave vines (cave vines + cave vines plant) (can't be harvested when locked)

3. Age-based crops and plants
- Wheat, carrots, potatoes, beetroots (can't be broken or trampled)
- Nether wart (can't be broken)
- Torchflower crop, pitcher crop (can't be broken or trampled)
- Cocoa (can't be broken)
- Sweet berry bush (can't be harvested/broken)
- Pumpkin stem, melon stem (prevents fruit spawn, but stems and fruit can still be broken (but trample prevented))
- Mangrove propagule (can still be broken)
- Notable: blocks age growth/bonemeal; locked stems also block melon/pumpkin fruit spawn.

4. Saplings and sapling-like growers - blocks sapling growth and tree generation (including bonemeal growth).
- Tree saplings: oak, spruce, birch, jungle, acacia, dark oak, cherry
- Azalea, flowering azalea

5. Mushrooms - blocks mushroom spread and huge mushroom generation (including bonemeal growth).
- Red mushroom, brown mushroom
